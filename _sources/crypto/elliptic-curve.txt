========================================
Elliptic Curve
========================================

根據 Vieta's formulas，
所有多項式的根的總和會等於 (-A[n-1]/A[n]) (the sum of the roots of any polynomial)，
其中 A[n] 是 leading coefficient。

* `Elliptic Curves as Elementary Equations <http://jeremykun.com/2014/02/10/elliptic-curves-as-elementary-equations/>`_
